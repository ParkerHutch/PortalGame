Name: Parker Hutchinson

Project Goal: 
	A level-based game where the player solves puzzles, trying to get to an objective in the level(like a door).
	The player is given a Portal-making tool to help them solve the puzzles and get to the end of the level. The
	player is able to enter through one portal and exit the other. 

Input/Commands: 
	
	Left-Click: Fire an orange portal
	Right-Click: Fire a blue portal

	Note: WASD commands alter the velocity of the player
	W: Move the player upwards
	A: Move the player downwards
	S: Move the player to the left
	D: Move the player to the right
